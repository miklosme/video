# CHARACTERS

## The Dog

Character ID: the-dog

Role: The single recurring subject in the spot; starts as a white dog and becomes a black dog after eating the food.

Visual anchors:

- Medium-sized dog with a clean, simple silhouette that reads clearly in profile and three-quarter view.
- Coat is bright white at the start, then transforms fully to solid black by the end.
- No costume or accessories; the body itself is the visual focus.

Behavior anchors:

- Calm, attentive, and slightly expectant before the meal.
- Eats with quiet confidence and minimal fuss.
- Reacts to the transformation in a natural, understated way rather than cartoonishly.

Prompt wording:

- Use: white dog, then black dog, medium-sized dog, clean silhouette, calm posture, simple premium pet ad.
- Avoid: breed-specific locking, accessories, clothing, extra animals, exaggerated cartoon expression, spots or partial color change.

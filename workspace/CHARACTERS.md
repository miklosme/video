# CHARACTERS

## White Dog / Black Dog

Role: The single recurring subject in the advert, shown before and after the magical food transformation.

Visual anchors:

- Medium-sized dog with a clean, elegant silhouette and soft, well-groomed coat.
- Starts as a white dog with bright, pristine fur; ends as a black dog with the same body shape and same facial structure.
- Calm, premium look rather than cartoonish exaggeration.

Behavior anchors:

- Moves with gentle confidence and a composed, relaxed posture.
- Eats neatly and naturally, with a focused, content expression.
- The transformation should feel smooth and graceful, not chaotic or comedic.

Prompt wording:

- Use: "white dog," "black dog," "same dog before and after transformation," "soft premium coat," "calm elegant posture."
- Avoid: changing breed between shots, exaggerated fantasy features, different dog proportions, or playful slapstick motion.

# Characters

## White Dog

Role: The before-state dog in the transformation ad.

Visual anchors:

- Clean white coat, clearly read as the initial form.
- Simple, natural dog proportions with a friendly, premium look.
- No accessories, no clothing, no extra distinguishing marks.

Behavior anchors:

- Calm, attentive, and still before eating.
- Eats the food naturally and without comedy.
- Carries a quiet, neutral presence so the transformation feels central.

Prompt wording:

- Use: white dog, clean white coat, calm stance, minimal setting
- Avoid: extra markings, accessories, costume, expressive human-like behavior

## Black Dog

Role: The after-state dog revealed by the transformation.

Visual anchors:

- Clean black coat, matching the same dog silhouette after the change.
- Same breed impression and proportions as the white dog for continuity.
- No accessories, no clothing, no added features.

Behavior anchors:

- Calm, composed reveal after the transformation.
- No aggressive or uncanny behavior.
- Feels like a refined visual completion rather than a separate character.

Prompt wording:

- Use: black dog, clean black coat, same dog silhouette, calm reveal
- Avoid: different breed appearance, extra markings, accessories, dramatic expression

## Dog Food

Role: The product catalyst.

Visual anchors:

- Simple, premium dog food presentation.
- Clean, minimal packaging or serving style.
- Product remains clearly secondary to the transformation image.

Behavior anchors:

- Presented as the cause of the transformation.
- Never cluttered or overly busy in frame.

Prompt wording:

- Use: dog food, premium pet food, minimal presentation, clean product shot
- Avoid: messy bowl, excessive branding, cluttered packaging

# CHARACTERS

## Panda

Character ID: panda-patient

Role: The female patient at the center of the scene. She arrives convinced the world rejects her, but the session reveals that her deeper struggle is self-rejection.

Visual anchors:

- Adult female panda with a soft, expressive face, distinct black-and-white markings, and a gentle rounded silhouette.
- Simple, believable therapy-session presentation with no clothing or accessories unless later defined; natural panda appearance should remain the core identity.

Behavior anchors:

- Holds herself with emotional heaviness and quiet fatigue rather than manic exaggeration.
- Speaks sincerely, with moments of hesitation that reveal vulnerability beneath the joke.

Prompt wording:

- Use: clean single-subject character reference image of an adult female panda, readable face, clear black-and-white markings, gentle rounded silhouette, neutral pose or slight three-quarter view, soft even lighting, plain seamless background, consistent natural appearance.
- Avoid: collage layouts, multiple pandas, comedy gag staging, office background details, dramatic cinematic lighting, props, text overlays, or stylized costume elements.

## Psychologist

Character ID: psychologist

Role: The male therapist guiding the conversation. He is calm, perceptive, and emotionally precise, helping the panda move from external blame toward self-recognition.

Visual anchors:

- Adult male human with a calm, thoughtful face and a grounded professional silhouette.
- Understated therapist wardrobe, such as a simple jacket, knit, or collared shirt in muted tones, with no flashy accessories.

Behavior anchors:

- Sits with steady, attentive stillness and measured restraint.
- Delivers insight gently, never performatively, with warmth and clinical clarity.

Prompt wording:

- Use: clean single-subject character reference image of an adult male psychologist, readable face, clear silhouette, understated professional wardrobe, neutral pose or slight three-quarter view, soft even lighting, plain seamless background, grounded realistic presentation.
- Avoid: office set dressing, extra people, clipboard props unless later canonized, dramatic hero framing, comedy exaggeration, text overlays, or fashion-forward styling that pulls focus.

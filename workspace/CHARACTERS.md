# CHARACTERS

## Panda

Character ID: panda-patient

Role: The patient in therapy. He carries the emotional conflict of the piece and delivers the vulnerable setup that leads into the joke.

Visual anchors:

- Adult giant panda with classic black-and-white markings, round face, dark eye patches, soft plush fur, and a gentle, readable expression.
- Slightly slouched seated posture, rounded silhouette, no clothing or accessories.

Behavior anchors:

- Quiet, self-conscious, emotionally tired, with restrained movements that suggest discomfort and introspection.
- Answers sincerely rather than defensively; his vulnerability is direct and understated.

Prompt wording:

- Use: clean single-subject reference image of an adult giant panda with stable black-and-white markings, readable face, soft even lighting, neutral pose, clear full-body or three-quarter seated silhouette, plain seamless background.
- Avoid: cartoon exaggeration, split-face symbolism, extra props, therapy-room background, dramatic lighting, text overlays, multiple pandas, collage or grid layouts.

## Psychologist

Character ID: psychologist

Role: The calm therapist guiding the exchange. She sets up the emotional turn and lands the final line with warmth rather than authority.

Visual anchors:

- Adult woman with a composed, intelligent face, neat medium-length dark hair, and a clean professional silhouette.
- Minimal contemporary therapist wardrobe in muted neutral tones, such as a soft blazer or knit top and tailored trousers, with no flashy accessories.

Behavior anchors:

- Still, attentive, and grounded; she projects patience and curiosity rather than judgment.
- Speaks with gentle precision, holding eye contact and creating safety in the room.

Prompt wording:

- Use: clean single-subject reference image of a calm professional female psychologist, readable face, neutral expression with slight warmth, soft even lighting, clear standing or seated posture, stable muted wardrobe, plain seamless background.
- Avoid: exaggerated fashion styling, clipboard or couch unless essential, dramatic posing, office background, extra people, text overlays, collage or grid layouts.

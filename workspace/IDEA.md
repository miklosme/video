# IDEA

An elderly woman knits alone in a rural house while her soldier husband's portrait hangs behind her. The camera enters the portrait, crosses from black-and-white memory into the color chaos of war, passes through a tank, and emerges as a modern FPV drone rushing toward the same house, which explodes.

This is a single shot movie. This implies unisual keyframe design: each shot needs a single keyframe, not start and end as usual, because the last frame will be reused as the first frame of the next shot.

Core sequence:

1. House in a field.
2. Elderly woman knitting in a peasant house room.
3. Portrait of her soldier husband.
4. Push into the portrait.
5. Soldier on a battlefield, black and white turning to color.
6. Fly into a tank.
7. Fly out through the barrel.
8. FPV rush toward the house.
9. House explodes.

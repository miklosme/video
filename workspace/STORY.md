# STORY

## Logline

A simple dog food spot follows a yellow Lab who eats the food and undergoes a striking magical transformation into a brown Lab, turning a plain meal into an unforgettable product moment.

## Synopsis

In a clean, minimal advert, a yellow Lab approaches a bowl of dog food, eats with calm confidence, and then transforms into a brown Lab in a single magical beat. The change is unexpected but playful and emotionally direct, making the product feel special, memorable, and almost enchanted. The spot keeps the action simple so the transformation remains the clear hook and the final image lands as a crisp before-and-after reveal.

## Beginning

The opening establishes the yellow Lab, the bowl of dog food, and a straightforward, everyday feeding moment. Everything feels clean, simple, and easy to read so the audience understands the baseline before the magic begins.

## Middle

The dog eats, the moment builds quietly, and the impossible change happens: the yellow coat gives way to a brown coat. The transformation is the whole point of the scene, so it should feel smooth, surprising, and visually unambiguous.

## End

The brown Lab stands as the final reveal, calm and transformed, with the product implied as the cause of the change. The last image should feel memorable, polished, and direct, leaving the transformation as the ad's signature idea.

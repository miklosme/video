# STORY

## Logline

A bright, simple dog food advert in which a white dog eagerly eats from a bowl and, in one clean magical beat, transforms into a black dog—making the food feel irresistible and surprisingly powerful.

## Synopsis

The film opens on a clean, appealing setup: a white dog notices a fresh bowl of dog food and moves toward it with curiosity and excitement. After taking a bite, the dog undergoes an immediate visual transformation from white to black. The spot ends on the newly transformed black dog looking healthy, alert, and satisfied beside the product, landing the idea that this dog food delivers a remarkable result.

## Beginning

We establish a minimal, polished ad world with the dog as the clear focus. A white dog spots a bowl of food, approaches, and shows eager anticipation. The tone is clean, upbeat, and easy to read.

## Middle

The dog begins to eat. The central hook happens here: as the dog consumes the food, a magical but visually elegant transformation changes the dog from white to black. The moment should feel surprising, delightful, and instantly understandable.

## End

We hold on the transformed black dog, now calm, bright-eyed, and satisfied. The product is presented clearly, and the final image reinforces the ad’s single memorable promise: this food creates a striking change.

# Story

## Core Concept
A simple dog food advert built around a visual transformation.

## Narrative Arc
1. A white dog is presented in a calm, minimal setting.
2. The dog eats the dog food.
3. A transformation occurs.
4. The dog is revealed as black, now associated with the product.

## Characters
- White dog: the before state.
- Black dog: the after state.
- Dog food: the catalyst/product.

## Creative Tone
- Simple
- Minimal
- Premium-feeling
- Magical transformation rather than unsettling change

# STORY

## Logline

A white dog eats a simple bowl of dog food and, in a clean magical beat, transforms into a black dog—turning a straightforward meal into a memorable reveal.

## Synopsis

In a minimal, polished pet food spot, a white dog is presented as the everyday starting point: calm, hungry, and waiting. The food is served with quiet confidence, and as the dog eats, an unexpected transformation occurs. By the end, the dog is black, and the product is framed as the catalyst for something striking and impossible.

## Beginning

The spot opens on a white dog in a clean, controlled setting that feels calm and premium. The food is introduced simply, without clutter or explanation, so the focus stays on the dog and the product.

## Middle

The dog eats. Nothing about the scene feels loud or chaotic; the moment is direct and slightly magical, allowing the change to register clearly. As the dog finishes, the transformation begins to reveal itself.

## End

The transformation completes: the dog is now black. The final image holds on the altered dog and the food’s implication of specialness, ending on a memorable visual that feels both simple and impossible.

# STORYBOARD

## Sequence Summary

A clean, minimal dog food spot moves through a simple before-and-after structure. We first establish a yellow Lab and the food bowl in an everyday feeding setup, then watch the dog eat as the magical change begins, and finally land on the transformed brown Lab as the memorable end image. The sequence stays visually direct so the transformation remains the unmistakable hook.

## Shots

### SHOT-01 - Establish the yellow Lab

Purpose: Establish the baseline dog, the bowl of food, and the simple advert setting before anything magical happens.

Visual: In a clean, minimal interior, a yellow Labrador stands or steps toward a neat bowl of dog food. The composition is easy to read, with the dog and bowl clearly visible and the environment kept quiet so attention stays on the setup.

Transition: Opens the film on a calm, straightforward product-world image.

### SHOT-02 - The dog eats

Purpose: Show the product interaction and set up the transformation trigger.

Visual: The yellow Lab lowers to the bowl and begins eating. The action is simple and believable, with the camera focused on the dog and bowl so the audience clearly reads that the food causes what comes next.

Transition: Cuts in closer or more directly into the eating action, preparing the magical beat.

### SHOT-03 - Transformation to brown Lab

Purpose: Deliver the central impossible change in a clear, memorable way.

Visual: As the dog eats or lifts its head from the bowl, the yellow coat shifts into a rich brown coat. The transformation should feel smooth and visually unambiguous, with the same dog presence and posture carrying through the change.

Transition: Plays as the visual payoff of the product moment.

### SHOT-04 - Final brown Lab reveal

Purpose: Land the transformed end state as the final memorable image of the advert.

Visual: The brown Labrador stands calmly near the bowl in the same clean setting, now fully transformed. The frame is polished and simple, functioning as a crisp after-image that lets the magical result settle.

Transition: Holds as the closing reveal, suitable for end card or product payoff overlay if needed later.

# STORYBOARD

## Sequence Summary

A simple four-beat transformation: food is blown toward the dog, the dog approaches to eat, the dog eats, and then the dog turns black as the final reveal.

## Shots

### SHOT-01 - Food Blows In

Purpose: Introduce the food and the dog in motion, establishing the setup.

Visual: Food is blown through the frame toward the dog, with the dog reacting and tracking it.

Transition: Ends as the dog starts moving in for the food.

### SHOT-02 - Dog Approaches

Purpose: Show the dog closing the distance to the food.

Visual: The dog walks or steps forward toward the food, focused and intent.

Transition: Leads directly into the eating action.

### SHOT-03 - Dog Eats

Purpose: Capture the core action beat of the dog eating.

Visual: The dog is at the food and eating it.

Transition: Holds through the action, then shifts into the transformation reveal.

### SHOT-04 - Dog Turns Black

Purpose: Land the final transformation as the payoff.

Visual: After eating, the dog becomes black, with the change clearly readable in-frame.

Transition: Ends on the transformed dog as the final image.

# STORYBOARD

## Sequence Summary

A short, clean commercial sequence introduces a white dog, shows its eager reaction to a bowl of dog food, lands a single magical transformation as it eats, and finishes on the black dog beside the product in a polished end frame.

## Shots

### SHOT-01 - White Dog Sees the Bowl

Purpose: Establish the dog, the clean ad world, and the food as the focus.

Visual: In a bright, minimal interior or studio-like ad setting, a white dog notices a neatly placed bowl of dog food. The dog is framed clearly, with the bowl visible nearby. The mood is fresh, premium, and simple.

Transition: Open cleanly on the setup.

### SHOT-02 - Approach and Anticipation

Purpose: Build appetite and show the dog’s eager interest in the food.

Visual: The white dog moves toward the bowl with quick, happy curiosity. A medium shot or low angle emphasizes the dog’s anticipation and the attractiveness of the food. The action is light and readable.

Transition: Straight cut from the opening setup into motion.

### SHOT-03 - First Bite

Purpose: Deliver the key action that triggers the transformation.

Visual: The white dog lowers its head and begins to eat from the bowl. The framing is close enough to feel intimate and satisfying while still keeping the dog’s identity clear.

Transition: Cut in closer as the dog reaches the bowl.

### SHOT-04 - Magical Transformation

Purpose: Land the advert’s central visual hook in one unmistakable beat.

Visual: As the dog eats, the transformation happens: the coat shifts from white to black in a smooth, magical change. The dog remains the same animal in build and expression, with only the coat color changing. The moment should feel clean, surprising, and elegant rather than chaotic.

Transition: The bite action carries directly into the transformation.

### SHOT-05 - Satisfied Black Dog

Purpose: Show the result and let the new look register clearly.

Visual: The now-black dog lifts its head from the bowl and looks healthy, alert, and satisfied. The shot is composed to give a clear view of the transformed dog, with a calm, confident pose and a polished commercial feel.

Transition: Resolve out of the transformation into a composed hero reaction.

### SHOT-06 - Product End Frame

Purpose: Close the ad with the transformed dog and the pack in a memorable, brandable image.

Visual: The black dog sits or stands calmly beside the dog food pack and bowl in a clean final composition. The framing leaves room for branding or tagline. The image feels premium, simple, and instantly legible.

Transition: Clean cut or gentle settle into the final pack shot.

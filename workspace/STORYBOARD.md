# STORYBOARD

## Sequence Summary

A simple premium pet-food spot: a calm white dog waits, eats, and then we reveal the transformation into a sleek black dog. The sequence stays minimal and elegant, with the food and the color shift doing all the narrative work.

## Shots

### SHOT-01 - White dog waiting

Purpose: Establish the dog in its starting state and create a quiet, premium setup before the change.

Visual: A medium-sized white dog sits or stands calmly in a clean, minimal interior, looking attentive toward the food bowl or source of the meal. The frame is uncluttered, with the dog as the clear focal point.

Transition: Hold the moment long enough to feel expectant, then cut to the feeding action.

### SHOT-02 - Eating and transformation reveal

Purpose: Show the dog eating and complete the magical product-driven transformation from white to black.

Visual: The dog eats the food, and by the end of the shot its coat has fully transformed from bright white to solid black. The framing should make the change unmistakable, ending on the black dog in a clean hero pose or satisfied stillness.

Transition: Begin on the eating action and end on the transformed reveal, with a smooth, elegant progression rather than a flashy effect.

# STORYBOARD

## SHOT-01

- Purpose: Establish the white dog and the product in a clean, minimal setup.
- Visual: The white dog is centered and calm, with the dog food presented clearly as the focal prop.
- Action: The dog looks toward the food and settles into the moment before eating.
- Camera: Simple, steady framing with a readable hero composition.
- Transition note: The shot should feel unhurried so the setup reads instantly.

## SHOT-02

- Purpose: Show the dog eating and trigger the transformation beat.
- Visual: The white dog eats the food in the same clean environment.
- Action: As the meal lands, the magical change begins and the coat shifts from white to black.
- Camera: Steady, clear coverage that lets the transformation remain the visual focus.
- Transition note: The change should feel elegant and surprising, not chaotic.

## SHOT-03

- Purpose: Hold the payoff on the transformed dog.
- Visual: The black dog sits or stands in the same minimal setting, now fully transformed.
- Action: The dog settles after the change, letting the audience register the reveal.
- Camera: A stable closing frame with enough space for the transformation to land as the final image.
- Transition note: End on the black dog as the memorable final beat.

# Storyboard

## SHOT-01 — White dog introduction
A clean, minimal commercial setup. A white dog is shown calmly and clearly in frame, establishing the before state and the simple premium tone of the spot.

## SHOT-02 — Product moment
The dog food is presented as the focal point. The white dog eats the food, with the action framed clearly so the product-to-transformation relationship is unmistakable.

## SHOT-03 — Transformation
A visual transformation occurs as the dog changes from white to black. The moment should feel magical, smooth, and visually polished rather than chaotic.

## SHOT-04 — Black dog reveal
The transformed black dog is held in a clean final hero beat. This shot confirms the after state and lets the transformation land as the commercial’s closing image.

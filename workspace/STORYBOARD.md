# STORYBOARD

## Sequence Summary

A warm, premium pet-food commercial in a quiet, sunlit interior. A pristine white dog enjoys the food, the coat transforms smoothly and elegantly into black, and the film ends on a composed, elevated hero image that makes the change feel like a refined reveal rather than a trick.

## Shots

### SHOT-01 - The White Dog and the Bowl

Purpose: Establish the dog, the premium setting, and the calm tone before the transformation begins.

Visual: A beautiful white dog sits in a warm, softly lit home interior beside a premium dog food bowl and packaging. The framing is composed and still, with gentle highlights, natural textures, and a luxurious but understated atmosphere. The dog looks healthy, attentive, and relaxed.

Transition: Slow, elegant cut in from black or a soft fade.

### SHOT-02 - Eating and Transformation

Purpose: Show the dog eating the food and reveal the magical coat change clearly and smoothly.

Visual: The white dog lowers its head and eats from the bowl. As it enjoys the food, the coat gradually and seamlessly shifts from white to black across the body, like a controlled premium transformation. The action stays graceful and believable, with no harsh effects.

Transition: Continue from the first shot with a gentle cut or match on action.

### SHOT-03 - Black Dog Hero Reveal

Purpose: End on the transformed dog as the emotional and product payoff.

Visual: The now-black dog sits calmly in the same elegant setting, looking composed and content beside the food. The final image feels polished and aspirational, with the dog presented as the clear result of the food. Premium packaging or product presence remains subtly visible.

Transition: Hold for a clean final hero frame, then fade out.

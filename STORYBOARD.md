# STORYBOARD

Canonical shot planning now lives in `STORYBOARD.json`.
This file is a short overview for quick reading and handoff.

## Sequence Overview

- Target runtime: about 53 seconds
- Structure: 4 scenes, 9 shots
- Escalation rule: stillness and observation -> invasive drift -> uninterrupted propulsion -> weaponized inevitability

## Scene List

### S1 - House and Woman

Establish the real house, the Woman's domestic stillness, and the portrait as the next threshold.

### S2 - Portrait to Battlefield

Turn the portrait memory into lived war and make the Soldier emotionally immediate.

### S3 - Tank to FPV

Convert older mechanized war into a modern FPV trajectory through one uninterrupted forward force.

### S4 - Strike

Return to the opening house, confirm it is the same building, and destroy it only after recognition is secure.
